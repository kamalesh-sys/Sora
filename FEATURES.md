# Sora Expense features

Sora Expense is a mobile-first Indian personal, home, and small-business money manager. It records income and expenses in one place while keeping daily entry fast.

## Accounts and privacy

- Account registration, login, logout, and persistent signed-in sessions.
- Human-verification protection during authentication.
- Private, user-scoped data with authenticated API access.
- Clear destructive-action confirmations for transactions, goals, people, and other records.

## Income and expenses

- One **Add transaction** flow for both expenses and income.
- A dedicated transaction type switch with clear positive and negative amounts throughout the app.
- Wallet balance calculated from lifetime personal income minus personal expenses.
- Monthly income, monthly expenses, and net cash flow on the dashboard and in reports.
- Fast amount entry in Indian rupees.
- Smart defaults for today, recent payment method, and recent category.
- Payment methods for UPI, Cash, Card, and Bank.
- Optional label and note fields, plus editable transaction dates.
- Edit and delete transactions with confirmation.
- Income is deliberately private; shared-money settlement stays expense-focused.

## Categories

- Separate category sets for income and expenses.
- Ready-made Indian daily-use expense categories and income categories such as Salary, Business, Freelance, Refund, and Gift.
- Create, edit, delete, and seed categories.
- Custom icons and colours.
- Reorderable category rail for one-handed, repeat entry.
- Last-used category remembered separately for income and expense entry.

## Dashboard and transactions

- Wallet balance and the selected month’s net cash flow.
- Monthly income and expense metrics.
- Budget status and recent mixed transaction activity.
- Quick access to Expense, Income, Goals, and People.
- Transaction list with type, payment, category, month, and sort filters.
- Signed amounts and colour cues so income and expenses stay distinguishable at a glance.
- Android home-screen widget support for the latest expense.

## Bills and budgets

- Monthly budget amount and optional note.
- Budget progress, remaining amount, and spent amount.
- Recurring bills with monthly, weekly, yearly, and custom schedules.
- Upcoming, paid, skipped, and overdue bill occurrences.
- Bill calendar/history and the ability to add or edit recurring bills.

## Goals

- Create a custom savings goal or start from a preset such as Emergency fund, Travel, Education, Home upgrade, Vehicle, Celebration, or Gadget.
- Goal colour selection and goal-specific iconography.
- Target amount, target date, saved amount, remaining amount, and required monthly contribution.
- Health states for on track, needs attention, overdue, and completed goals.
- Contribution history, optional expense-linked contributions, month skipping, and restoring skipped months.
- Goal editing and deletion with accurate refreshes after every action.

## Loans

- Separate borrowed and lent-money records, so loans never distort ordinary income or expense totals.
- Track the counterparty, original principal, loan type, disbursed date, repayment frequency, planned payment, due date, maturity date, and status.
- Optional simple annual interest, agreement/reference number, account or UPI reference, collateral, repayment terms, and private notes.
- A repayment ledger with payment date, method, reference, note, and an auditable principal, interest, and fee split.
- Automatic repayment allocation applies outstanding interest before principal; manual allocation is available when a lender statement differs.
- Outstanding principal, accrued interest, paid totals, repayment progress, due-state, and complete repayment history in one place.

## People and shared spending

- Add people and track whether they owe you or you owe them.
- Record shared expenses and settlement payments.
- Person balance summaries and individual history.

## Reports and exports

- Monthly income, expense, net cash-flow, budget, and wallet summaries.
- Category and payment-method breakdowns for both transaction types.
- CSV and PDF report export.

## Personalisation and usability

- Light, dark, and system theme modes.
- Preset and custom accent colours.
- English, Tamil, and Hindi layouts.
- Responsive mobile-first spacing, large touch targets, accessible labels, loading states, empty states, error states, and pull-to-refresh on data screens.
- Android APK download page with consent, download progress, privacy information, terms, and installation guidance.
